Description: Compute sum of the first ten powers of e

“Compute and print out the first ten powers of e and their sum.”

    e = 2.71828
    i = 0
    sum = 0
    
l1  next = e ** i
    output = 'e^' i ' = ' next
    sum = sum + next
    i = i + 1
    lt(i, 10)      :s(l1)

    output = 'Sum of first ten powers of e: ' sum

end
